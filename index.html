Baldur's Gate 3 - Semana do Zero ao Programador Contratado
WIZARD

Gale

Gale of Waterdeep is a wizard prodigy whose love for the goddess of magic made him attempt a dread feat no mortal should. Blighted by the forbidden magic of ancient Netheril, Gale strives to undo the corruption that is overtaking him and win back his goddess favour before he becomes a destroyer of worlds.

FIGHTER

Lae'zel

Lae'zel was raised ready for a life amongst the stars, mercilessly conquering the cosmos as a githyanki soldier. Grounded, she must deal with a world she doesn't understand, and find a way to serve her people in a plane that despises her militant kin. Will Lae'zel prove herself to Queen Vlaakith, or must she walk another path in exile?

ROGUE

Astarion

Astarion was born in the Year of the Carrion Crow, but in the Year of Daystars, he was attacked by a group of Gurs. He was rescued by Cazador Szarr, who turned him into his vampire spawn for centuries and forced him to follow every of his sadistic orders. Free for now, Astarion will do anything to keep his life in the light and become many times more powerful than his old abuser could ever dream of being.

BARBARIAN

Karlach

Fresh-escaped from Hell, Karlach is finally free of the archdevil Zariel - but not from the infernal engine Zariel planted in her chest. Zariel is the ruler of Avernus and forced Karlach serve as a soldier to fight the blood war for ten years. With her first taste of freedom, she is eager to find a fix for the engine that's burning hotter and hotter before it burns her out completely.

CLERIC

Shadowheart

A loyal cleric of Shar, goddes of darkness and loss, Shadowheart is the sole survivor of a holy mission undertaken on the Mistress of the Night's behest. She alone must deliver a relic of immense power to her coven in Baldur's Gate, while threatened by a strange new magic that is burgeoning from within. She must complete her mission successfully to win Shar's love and have her memories restored.

WARLOCK

Wyll

Wyll made his name as a renowned hero and a monster hunter called Blade of Frontiers in Baldur's Gate. But to become a living legend, he struck a bargain with a devil named Mizora and always kept the source of his power a secret. Mizora only asks Wyll to sacrifice devilish creatures to her, but a cambion's ambitions are ever fickle, and Wyll wishes to escape the pact before its price grows cruel and consumes his soul for good.
